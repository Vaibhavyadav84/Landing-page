Features

Adidas Originals ZX 750

The Adidas Originals ZX 750 is a popular sneaker model that belongs to the Adidas ZX series, which originated in the 1980s. Known for its retro style and comfortable fit, the ZX 750 features a combination of suede, mesh, and synthetic materials in its upper construction, providing both durability and breathability.

Adidas Originals Forum '84 High

The Adidas Originals Forum '84 High is a classic high-top sneaker that pays homage to its 1984 debut as a basketball shoe. Known for its distinctive retro style, the Forum '84 High features a premium leather upper, ankle strap for added support, and perforated toe box for breathability. The shoe combines vintage design elements with modern comfort, including a cushioned midsole and durable rubber outsole.

Adidas Originals Superstar 2 II Leather Trainers in Black (model G17067)

The Adidas Originals Superstar 2 II Leather Trainers in Black (model G17067) are a modern take on the iconic Superstar sneaker that debuted in the 1960s. Known for their distinctive shell toe, these trainers feature a sleek black leather upper, providing a premium look and feel. The classic rubber shell toe cap adds durability and protection, while the three white stripes and heel tab offer a striking contrast. The shoe also includes a cushioned insole and rubber outsole with a herringbone pattern for enhanced grip and comfort.

About Us

Adidas Originals is a line of casual and sportswear products by Adidas, inspired by the brand's heritage and its archives. Launched in 2001, Adidas Originals combines classic and contemporary designs, celebrating the brand's history while appealing to modern fashion trends. The line includes iconic footwear such as the Superstar, Stan Smith, and Gazelle sneakers, as well as apparel and accessories. Known for its distinctive Trefoil logo, Adidas Originals has become synonymous with street style and youth culture, blending performance and style seamlessly. Through collaborations with designers, artists, and celebrities, Adidas Originals continues to innovate and maintain its relevance in the ever-evolving fashion landscape.
